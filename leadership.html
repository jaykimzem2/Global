Founder & Principal

Peter
Wairegi

Peter Wairegi is not what the industry typically produces. He is what decades of real, high-stakes market experience creates — a strategist whose instincts have been forged in the markets of the United States, refined through international development advisory, and sharpened through the discipline of academic teaching.

His career began at Century 21 in the United States in the 1980s — an era when real estate marketing was a discipline of persuasion, relationships, and positioning, long before the digital age introduced the illusion that reach equals results.

He has sold over 20,000 residential units and thousands of commercial properties. He has managed loan portfolios exceeding $1.5 billion. He has sat across the table from developers, investors, and institutions — and he has learned what moves capital, and why.

A devoted student of Brian Tracy, Peter's approach to marketing follows the architecture of trust — rooted in relationship, elevated through insight, and consummated through long-term value creation for everyone involved.
